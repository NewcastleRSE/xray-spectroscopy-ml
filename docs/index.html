XANESNET is an open-source toolbox for the rapid and automated analysis and prediction of X-ray spectroscopy data. The software is collaboratively developed by the Penfold Group and the Research Software Engineering (RSE) team at Newcastle University.

This documentation provides a guide to interacting with and contributing to XANESNET.
